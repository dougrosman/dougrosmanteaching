**Body:**
Hello all!

This is Doug Rosman (one of your graduate projects advisors), and I’m reaching out to say hello, and to start coordinating our meeting times for the upcoming Fall 2025 semester.

**Graduate Advising will begin in Week 2 of the semester, in the first week of September.**

 Time slot sign-ups for my section will become available one week from today on **Friday, August 22 at 10:00am CST**. This is a "first-come, first-served" system, so the sooner you sign up, the more options you'll have. I will send out a Google Calendar invitation for the sign up time to remind you.

Watch this 2-minute video on how to sign up: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EpOuzBPBUNQ

**How to sign up for a time slot:**
1. Go to your Canvas Calendar ([https://saic.instructure.com/calendar](https://saic.instructure.com/calendar))
2. Select "Find Appointments"
3. Select our section and press "Submit"
4. Find the open time slots in the first week of September
5. Select a time slot and enter the following into the comment box:
	1. Preferred name
	2. Pronouns (optional)
	3. Preferred meeting location (e.g. MacLean 1011) (Note: we can change locations each week as needed, just put your primary location here for now. If you would like to meet regularly in my office, my office is MacLean 405)
6. Click 'Reserve'

Once you sign up for a time slot, I will send you a recurring Google Calendar invitation for your time slot that will appear in both of our calendars.

**More information:**
- We will meet every other week during your time slot for approximately 50 minutes.
- Meetings will end on time.
- We will meet up to 7 times in the semester, with a minimum of 5 meetings in order to receive credit.
- If you want to meet somewhere other than your preferred location, email me ahead of time so I know where to go.

**Rescheduling policy:**
- In general, rescheduling is discouraged; *treat our time slot like any other class in your schedule.*
- If you need to reschedule, please let me know as soon as possible, ideally at least 24 hours in advance of our meeting. I can't guarantee I'll be able to reschedule within the same or following week.
- For those of you who have advised with me in the past, I know I have been rather accommodating with rescheduling. This semester, I have a more rigid schedule and will be less flexible, but I will do what I can.

**Cancellation/no-show policy:**
- If I show up to our meeting and you're not there, and I have not heard from you, I'll wait 15 minutes before I leave.
