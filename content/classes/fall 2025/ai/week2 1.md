Following what we did in class, create a large-scale intricate composition made possible through advanced inpainting techniques.

You may work with your partner, or create a composition of your own outside of class.


**Follow these steps whether or not you're working with a partner.**

Step 0: **Brainstorm** | Based on the images we looked at, consider the type of scene you want to create. Consider the scale of your location, and what elements you want to include. Do some writing for a few minutes. Imagine the overall scene, then think of what types of objects or imagery you want to populate that scene with. Essentially, consider your vision *before you sit down in front of the computer.*
